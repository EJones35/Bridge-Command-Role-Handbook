Captain

Main Role

The captain is the role with the highest say on the ship. However, this does not mean they have absolute power - that is what the detention bay is for. The captain should:

  1. Listen to the crew
  2. Manage tasks
The captain should not:
  1. Take over stations
  2. Ruin someone else's time - they paid the same money you did for the experience
